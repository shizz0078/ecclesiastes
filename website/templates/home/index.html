Meet The Team

Monique Harrington, a Philly native, received Summa Cum Laude honors from Northeastern University along with her Bachelor’s degree in Entrepreneurship and Small Business Management. With a desire to see a better world, she set out to apply her business education and organizational skills as an administrative support and resource for nonprofit and small businesses.

Over ten years, Monique has demonstrated advanced proficiency in a broad skill set with basic administrative experience as well as complex program development and management. Executive leadership, human resources, finances, marketing, development, information technology, and direct program work are vital skills that Monique carries into every meeting and consultation. Her ability to develop processes and staffing procedures has demonstrated boosts to efficiency and effectiveness in both large and small organizations. Most importantly, Monique has a unique ability to work with people, support their visions, and make them feel valued and respected. Her honesty, reliability, integrity, and perseverance are the inherent talents that make her practice work.


Virtual Administrative Assistance

Delegate administrative tasks to our devoted team and access a reliable, efficient resource to handle specific tasks. The time that your organization saves in task delegation frees up resources for serving your clients and community.

Small Business/Nonprofit Consultation

Following an in-depth consultation meeting to discuss organization goals and current operational procedures, client will receive a written assessment report that outlines recommended steps to effectively implement strategic goals and boost operational efficiency. Our assessment will also identify the tasks that can be delegated to a virtual administrative assistance service to save valuable resources.
